## Tips for Consistency

### 1. Set a Weekly Reminder
- **When:** Sunday 8pm or Monday 9am
- **Calendar event:** "WGO Metrics Collection (7 min)"
- **Recurring:** Weekly

### 2. Use Browser Bookmarks
Create a folder called "WGO Metrics" with:
- Substack Dashboard
- X Analytics  
- LinkedIn Analytics
- Bluesky Profile

### 3. Track Trends, Not Just Numbers
The **change** matters more than the absolute number:
- Growing steadily? Good.
- Sudden spike? What caused it?
- Plateau? Time to experiment.

### 4. Connect to Your Growth Doc Goals
Review your targets monthly:
- **Q1 End (March 31):** Substack 750, X 2,000, LinkedIn 1,000
- **Q2 End (June 30):** Substack 2,000, X 5,000, LinkedIn 1,500
